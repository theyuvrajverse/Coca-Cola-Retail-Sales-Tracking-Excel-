# Coca-Cola USA Retailer Performance Dashboard (Excel)

## Business Problem

Retail and FMCG companies require continuous visibility into sales growth, profitability, retailer performance, and brand contribution to make informed business decisions. This project analyzes Coca-Cola's retail sales performance across major U.S. retailers, brands, and time periods to identify growth drivers and profitability trends.

The objective was to build an interactive business intelligence dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor year-over-year performance, evaluate retailer effectiveness, and compare brand-level growth.

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains Coca-Cola retail sales information across multiple:

* Retailers
* Beverage Brands
* Regions
* Time Periods
* Sales Channels

Key metrics analyzed include:

* Sales Revenue
* Operating Profit
* Year-over-Year Growth
* Brand Performance
* Retailer Performance

## Dashboard Development

Built an interactive Excel dashboard featuring:

* Sales Trend Analysis
* Year-over-Year Variance Tracking
* Retailer Performance Comparison
* Brand-Level Performance Analysis
* Profitability Monitoring
* Dynamic Filtering and Drill-Down Analysis

## Dashboard Metrics

* Sales Revenue (2022): ₹24,23,733
* Sales Revenue (2023): ₹95,92,933
* Operating Profit: ₹47,22,497
* Nearly 4× year-over-year sales growth

## Key Findings

* Total sales increased from ₹24,23,733 in 2022 to ₹95,92,933 in 2023, representing nearly fourfold growth.
* Operating profit reached ₹47,22,497, demonstrating strong overall business performance.
* Coca-Cola and Diet Coke delivered the highest positive sales variance among all brands.
* Sprite and Powerade recorded comparatively smaller year-over-year growth.
* Amazon and BevCo generated the largest retailer-level sales variance, highlighting the growing importance of high-performing retail channels.
* Revenue growth was not evenly distributed across brands, indicating stronger consumer demand for specific products.
* Retailer performance analysis revealed significant differences in contribution to total sales growth.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase investment in high-growth retail partners such as Amazon and BevCo.
* Expand promotional efforts around Coca-Cola and Diet Coke, which demonstrated the strongest growth momentum.
* Investigate opportunities to improve sales performance for lower-growth brands such as Sprite and Powerade.
* Continue monitoring retailer-specific performance to optimize distribution and marketing strategies.
* Leverage profitability insights to prioritize high-margin product and retailer combinations.
